Welcome to the Library Management System

Manage your library with ease. Here are some features you can explore:

Manage Categories

Create and organize different categories for books. This helps in better sorting and finding books based on genres or subjects.

Manage Authors

Add and update information about authors. Keep track of all the authors whose works are available in the library.

Manage Publishers

Maintain a list of publishers who provide books to the library. This includes their contact information and the books they publish.

Manage Books

Add new books to the library collection, update existing entries, and manage book details such as title, author, category, and publisher.

Browse Library

Search for books, authors, publishers, and categories. Browse the entire collection and discover new and interesting books.
